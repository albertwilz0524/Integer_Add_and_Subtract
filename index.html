New Concept!

When the second number is negative, the original direction of movement changes!

For example, in 5 + (-2), supposedly you need to go to the right since the operation is add, but you will instead move to the left since the second number is negative.

Similarly, for -2 - (-5), supposedly you need to go to the left since the operation is subtract, but you will instead move to the right!
